Almot: A Smart Helmet for Enhancing Road Information Perception

Product Design

In recent years, electric bicycles have spread rapidly in China. However, the popularity of electric bicycles has also led to frequent occurrences of related traffic accidents. Electric bike cyclists who have already complied with the traffic rules, also endure a lot of risks when riding on the road, due to two main problems: limited vision and information interference. Therefore, aiming at enhancing the perception of traffic information for electric bicycle riders, we proposed ALMOT, a helmet design that is smart and modular.
