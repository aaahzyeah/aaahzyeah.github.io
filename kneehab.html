Kneehab: A System for Self- Monitoring Knee Overstretching

Product Design

In response to the knee overextension problem that often occurs during lower limb rehabilitation, we proposed a smart system to motivate users and provide a data-driven communication platform for therapists and patients. We have adopted a user-centric design method, in-depth research on wearable sensing technology in hardware, and iterative design through in-depth cooperation with users and experts on the software interfaces. In the end, we conducted technical testing and user testing, which obtained high accuracy and user satisfaction.
